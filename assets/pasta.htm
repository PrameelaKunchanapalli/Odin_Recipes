PASTA

Description

This creamy white sauce pasta is so rich and velvety,it should come with a warning:'May cause uncontrollable cravings!' Drenched in a luscious, garlicky, cheesy hug,every bite melts in your mouth like a love story between butter and milk.

Ingredients

  • 1 package cavatappi pasta
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 spoon butter
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, diced
  • 1 spoon pepper powder
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 large tomato, diced
  • 2 capsicum , diced
  • 1 cup grated cheese
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons milk

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook until tender yet firm to the bite,about 8 to 10 minutes.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, melt butter with olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add shallot, garlic, and pepper; cook and stir until lightly browned and fragrant, about 2 minutes. Remove from the heat.
  3. Bring cream to a simmer in a saucepan along with 3 tablespoons of milk over medium heat. Stir in shallot mixture and tomato, then mix in flour and black pepper. Simmer until thickened, 5 to 8 minutes. Stir in Parmesan cheese. Remove from the heat and let sit until pasta is finished.
  4. Drain pasta. Serve pasta on plates and ladle sauce over top.
