Coffee, aromatic, and pure magic in a cup!☕✨ From the first whiff to the last sip, it awakens the soul, fuels dreams. Whether it’s a strong espresso or a creamy latte, Hug in a Mug

Ingredients

  • 1 cup hot brewed coffee
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk

Steps

  1. Whisk boiling water, instant coffee, and sugar together until coffee is dissolved. Continue to whisk until mixture becomes thick and forms a peak, 2 to 3 minutes.
  2. Pour milk into a glass. Pour the frothy coffee on top of the milk.
  3. Hug in a mug is ready.
